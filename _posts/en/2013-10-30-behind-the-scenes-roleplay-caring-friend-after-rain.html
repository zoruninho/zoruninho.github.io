I hesitated a lot before doing this video. I wanted to make one to thanks all my subscribers but also wanted to make one with more triggers than the last videos.

Finally, I decided to make this roleplay, with more triggers and some thanks "hidden" inside !

My set-up to record was like this :

- Two towels on the ground to avoid to my knees a slow death (which happened during the recording of the Hair Salon roleplay)
- An Ipod which will be used as a mirror after the hair drying
- Some beautiful slippers on the right which have absolutely nothing to do here.

Let's look a little closer :

The brush and the hanky are here for the head massage, the microphone isn't in the camera's field of view, all seems to be ok for a normal recording !

But I forgot the telephone, located in my room, which can ring at any moment because of commercial calls. And it happened of course !

I hate it so much. It looks so harmless and peaceful like this but it loves to ring always at the worse moments. Today, this phone decided that I have slept enough and rang to help me to wake up. So nice.

But, after this little problem solved, all went well and I hope that you liked/will like the video !
