Recently, I didn't have much time to record an ASMR video. Or should I say that I didn't have much freedom for that because I never was alone at home and I think this is disturbing to record some ASMR. So, when I knew that I will have the home for myself during some days, I planned to make as many vids as possible.

But first, I really needed to cut my hair. I wasn't seeing much anymore without moving them from in front my eyes and my head was feeling so heavy. So I went to my usual hair saloon and got this vital haircut. That was really relaxing and that made me remember that I never tried to record a binaural haircut yet. When I went back at home, I started to think about it.

Then, I needed a good place. It was still early in the afternoon and that was magnificently sunny outside. My first idea was to record behind my glass door, with the camera showing my garden and a little mirror showing my head while cutting the hair. That could have been really great but here is what I saw :

[caption id="attachment_1111" align="aligncenter" width="800"] Oh yes, recording against the light is not that great, right[/caption]

I thought about recording outside but that was hard to do with the wind sound or the neighboors. Finally, I had to change my plans and only record the inside of my house, but I kept the idea of the mirror and prepared my recording place like this :

[caption id="attachment_1113" align="aligncenter" width="800"] Nice flower tablecloth isn't it?[/caption]

If you are wondering, or even if you are not, yes, this solid wood furniture is not here usually. And yes, this is quite heavy! But that was the best thing to record, I love the appearance of the wood on a video, and that was exactly the height I was researching to put the mirror on it. Thanks to that, my camera was able to record my face while I was giving the haircut.

[caption id="attachment_1114" align="aligncenter" width="800"] Zoruception[/caption]

When all my devices were ready, I thought about what I will use to make the sounds. The scissors sounds are easy, I just need scissors. Brush sounds are possible with a brush and a face flannel. But I wanted to give a scalp massage, with the sensation of the wet hair. So I tried with a sponge on a bath towel, that was awful. I was thinking again about it, and while doing that, I was pressing the sponge and I heard the sounds of it in my earphones. That was exactly what I wanted and I kept the sponge as the soundmaker.

Finally, I wanted to make some spray sounds like in my first haircut video. But in this one, I used a perfume sample to make the sounds and that was really hard to look as normal as possible when the bathroom was smelling perfume as if the product was being created right here. But I don't have any water spray. So I took back the perfume sample and cleaned it with water and fIlling it again with some fresh water. There wasn't many perfume left in the sample before I cleaned it but my bathroom is still covered with it's smell.

[caption id="attachment_1116" align="aligncenter" width="800"] Winners team[/caption]

Then, the recording started and all went nicely. But spraying some perfumed water on my hand disturbed my dog when he saw me again after the vid. He snorted me constantly, wondering why I was smelling that :D

This was a really pleasant recording to do, I hope you liked or will like the video and that you enjoyed this behind the scenes article!

Thank you all for the support, see you soon!

Quand tous mes appareils furent prêts, je me mis à réfléchir à ce que j'allais utiliser pour faire les sons. Les sons de ciseaux sont faciles à produire, il suffit d'avoir des ciseaux. Les sons de brosse sont possibles avec une brosse et un gant de toilette. Mais je voulais donner un massage crânien avec la sensation que les cheveux étaient mouillés. J'ai donc essayé avec une éponge sur une serviette de bain, c'était horrible. Je continuais alors à réfléchir, et tout en réfléchissant, je pressais l'éponge et j'entendais ses sons dans mes écouteurs. C'était exactement ce que je recherchais et je garda l'éponge pour produire ces sons.

Enfin, je voulais faire des sons de spray comme dans ma première vidéo de coupe de cheveux. Mais dans cette dernière, j'utilisais un échantillon de parfum et c'était particulièrement difficile d'avoir l'air normal alors que la salle de bain sentait aussi fort que si le produit était créé dans la pièce. Mais je n'ai pas de spray à eau. J'ai donc de nouveau pris l'échantillon de parfum mais l'ai cette fois-ci lavé à l'eau avant de le remplir d'eau fraîche. Il n'y avait quasiment pas de parfum dans l'échantillon avant que je ne le nettoie mais la salle de bain est encore envahie par son odeur à l'heure où j'écris.

C'était un enregistrement très agréable à faire, j'espère que vous avez aimé ou allez apprécier la vidéo et cet article !
