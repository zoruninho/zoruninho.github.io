I usually sit during my videos, but it would have been inappropriate for this one. So I decided to record while I was standing up.

This wasn't a problem at all, but my recording location was. I didn't want to use my usual place for this kind of exam, And there isn't a lot of places where I can stand up and have a good light in my house so I didn't have many choices.

Finally I decided to use the room which is next to mine. I never recorded there because the wall isn't very pretty and I only have one place where I can record if I want enough lights. And this place is necessarily against that ugly wall. That's why I put the near card on it, to make it less empty and cold.

Below you can see my magnificent ironing board. You can't see it on the vid but I'm certain that it makes the recording a lot better.
