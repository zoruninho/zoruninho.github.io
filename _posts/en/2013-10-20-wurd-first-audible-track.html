--- title: "Wurd - First Audible Track" idName: "musicWurd" categories: "music" lang : en ---

I won't post on this website any "music" I made before Wurd. There is a good reason to that, it's mostly very bad stuff from my learning period. I'm still learning a lot at each session, and the global quality is not so good, but it's far away from these first tries.

But one night came Wurd from the middle of nowhere. It was like at 1am, I was hesitating between doing something or going to sleep, and I finally decided to launch my FL Studio program. After 2 hours of non-stop producing, Wurd was born. The beginning of the song is quite bad, but this is clearly my sound with the most unique "atmosphere". When I listen to it, I can imagine a short scene with someone running away from someone else. The same feeling was shared by a friend.

It's still far from perfect, far from pretty good, but I think it's my favorite sound.
