During my first year of studying in my UIT, we had to create a little video game based on a board game for kids. I may talk about it in another post in my project category !

With my partner, we finished earlier than expected, so I took a little of my free time to create some musics for the game. The main one is here : Turtles.

I made it while thinking about old video game themes, and chose what I thought to be an adequate synthesizer. I hope you will like this one !
