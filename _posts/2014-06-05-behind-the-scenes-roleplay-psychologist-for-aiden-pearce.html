Long time since my last vid so when I learned that I'll be alone at home for 1 hour, I rushed to make a new one!

So I had the whole house for myself. But that wasn't expected so I didn't know what to record. A roleplay? A sound-only? Where?

And when I was thinking about that, I was still holding the xbox360 controller linked to my computer. I was playing Watch Dogs and that gave me an idea. I don't especially like the game main character, I think he is creating a lot of troubles and that he is not caring at all about others people life. What he only cares about is his family, that's good to care about it but he doesn't have to steal and kill innocent citizens meanwhile!

I know that's a game and I'm enjoying it, and I'm mainly joking about Aiden Pearce, the main character, but I thought that it could be a good idea to make him encounter a psychologist after potentially being arrested.

[caption width="800" align="alignnone"] I'm saving my family by killing grandmothers[/caption]

So let's imagine that Aiden Pearce is arrested. And let's roleplay his psychologist. I knew that it probably won't be understood by many people because you have to know the game to fully understand the vid, but I really wanted to do it anyway. I tried to be very relaxing in order to make it at least enjoyable for all of you, even if you maybe can't understand it.

When the idea was clear in my head, I grabbed my iPhone and ... watched the 1% battery left with a livid face. I only use my iPhone for recording since I changed my phone but I can't use it when I need it.

The problem was : my new phone (LG G2) is great for recording but my old tripod is not large enough to handle this one. I already tried a few times and that won't fit. So I managed to make it stand with my old-school methods as show below :

[caption id="attachment_1142" align="aligncenter" width="768"] A book, a tic-tac box, who needs more?[/caption]

And let's get started!

This was the first recording I did only with one shot. I launched my recorders, I yelled "TOP" and beginned to think about what I'll say. Because, yes, I didn't think about it before starting to record, I was so short in time and concentrated on placing my devices.

But all went correctly, I improvised the dialogs that could occur between Aiden and a psychologist and I had fun doing it!

I hope that some of you may enjoy this video, see you soon for a new one!
