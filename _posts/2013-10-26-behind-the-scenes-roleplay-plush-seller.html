For this first Behind The Scenes post, I will talk about a recent video and the steps of its production !

As you can see in the image ahead, I have to prepare all the stuff I will use in a video. But it's easy. On the other side, placing the camera and the microphone is not.

I have to place the microphone close to me, but it mustn't be visible in the video so I have to play with the angles for several minutes before shooting.

When everything is ready, I launch Audacity to record the audio and my video recording can begin !

During this recording session, all went fine and I can save the audio kindly prepared by audacity.

Before processing the audio and make some modifications to it, I have to copy the recorded video into my computer.

When the sound is correctly modified, I start the editing of the final vid !

After all that work, which can take 30 minutes or several hours depending on the video, I launch the rendering and then upload the result on youtube.
